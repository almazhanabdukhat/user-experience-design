My Experiences

I have recently completed an internship at a consulting firm in NY where I've built an app that calculates Daily Light Integral, a value that indicates how much light a plant receives at a particular date and location. Calculating DLI can help to select when and which crops to grow at the chosen location based on how much light a plant receives and what cover type for buildings is used.

In January, I was a fellow at a TavTech technology fellowship in Tel-Aviv (sponsored by NYU), where I learnt about cybersecurity, Israeli start-up scene and ate lots of falafels. We also had visits to local tech start-ups and big corporate offices (i.e. Google).

I have recently visited Louvre Abu Dhabi in the UAE as I studied away at NYU's Abu Dhabi campus - I really enjoyed the experience and learnt quite a bit about local modern art. Louvre Abu Dhabi had exhibits from the original Louvre and museums all around the world.
